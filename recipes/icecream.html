Strawberry ice cream

Description

Strawberry ice cream is super easy to make with this recipe that doesn't use an egg-custard base but still tastes rich and creamy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cartons of sour cream
  • 1 Can of condensed milk
  • 1 Strawberry box

Steps

  1. Beat the strawberries in a blender.
  2. Open the cream boxes and put in a jar.
  3. Put the shaken strawberries.
  4. Mix until smooth.
  5. Put the condensed milk and mix.
  6. Cover the pot and take it to the freezer.
