Carrot cake

Description

Easy to make carrot cake

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups of wheat flour
  • 3 Eggs
  • 2 Cups sugar
  • 3 Medium carrots (chopped and peeled)
  • 1 Cup oil
  • 1 Tablespoon (soup) baking powder

Steps

  1. Place the carrot, sugar, oil and eggs in a blender, beat until the carrot undoe
  2. Place in a bowl and mix the wheat flour and baking powder.
  3. Pour into a rectangular baking sheet.
  4. Bake in medium oven, preheated, for approximately 35 minutes.
