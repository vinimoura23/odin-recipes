Homemade Lasagna

Description

a delicious and easy to prepare lasagna

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 cloves of garlic mashed
  • 1 chopped small onion
  • Oregano to taste
  • Olive oil to braise
  • 1 Can of tomato paste of 340g
  • 2 cubes of meat broth
  • 1 Pack of 200g pre-cooked lasagna
  • 250g sliced ham
  • Grated cheese for sprinkling
  • 250g sliced mozzarella

Steps

  1. Hide the sour and refute the onion and alho.
  2. Add the ground beef, 1 cup of water and cook until cooked.
  3. Place the tomato extract, oregano and about 1 and 1/2 liters of mare.
  4. Leave about 10 minutes and take off, acrescent salsinha and zaolinha.
  5. The sauce should be very liquid.
  6. Line the bottom of a refractory with some of the sauce.
  7. Alternate layers of lasagna dough, sauce, ham slices, mozzarella slices.
  8. The last layer should be mozzarella.
  9. Sprinkle grated cheese.
  10. Take it in the oven and let the mossarela melt.
